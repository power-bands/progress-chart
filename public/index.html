How to Use This Tool

This tool produces a schedule of completion dates for each chapter in a book. You provide the number of chapters, pages in each chapter, and how many pages per day (PPD) you'll read.

Currently, if the number of pages in a chapter is not evenly divisble by the PPD, the scheduled completion date is rounded up to the next full day. Due to this, the actual average PPD can be lower than the amount you provide.

Completion dates are scheduled inclusively beginning at the current date.

Bullet Journal Users

The resulting chart is designed for transcription into your bullet journal. It includes a few extra columns you might find useful for tracking your progress and referencing related collections:

  • Start Column

    # No S F P D T V

    Mark the date you begin reading the target chapter.

  • Finish Column

    # No S F P D T V

    Mark the date you finish reading the target chapter.

  • Duration Column

    # No S F P D T V

    Mark the number of days you spent reading the target chapter.

  • Transcription Column

    # No S F P D T V

    Mark the page numbers of any transcribed passages you made from the target chapter.

  • Vocabulary Column

    # No S F P D T V

    Mark the page numbers of any vocabulary collections derived from the target chapter.
